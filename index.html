Services

Salconn stands for Student Alumni Connect. As the name suggests, we aim to bridge the communication gap between a high school student about to enter the undergrad world and the Alumni of an institute who has experienced it all. More often than not, students tend to make the wrong decisions based on the little information they have about their target institutes. They are either misinformed about the college placements or the specific branch of study they are applying for. They join the college and deep-down regret their decisions. We know it because we have experienced the same. But it’s always too late. We believe that this should be avoided at all costs because at the end of the day, it’s one of the biggest life decisions a student will make. Here comes Salconn. We will make sure the decision is calculated and strategic and that the student knows every granular detail about the college they’re headed to. We will set up a one on one communication with an advisor YOU chose from a list WE provide. Worry not. Our advisors are not expensive counselors who have a vague idea about every college in India. Our advisors are the Alumni of your intended college who have seen it all and are in a position to guide you. They will be from your target college and the branch you’re probably confused about. They will tell you every little detail about the future and make sure you make the right decision. You deserve to know from someone who is qualified enough and we will make sure of that.
