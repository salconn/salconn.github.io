About

SALCONN stands for Student Alumni Connect. We aim to bridge the communication gap between a high school student about to enter the undergrad world and the Alumni of an institute who has experienced it all. More often than not, students tend to make the wrong decisions based on the little information they have about their target institutes. They are either misinformed about the college placements or the specific branch of study they are applying for. We believe that this should be avoided at all costs because at the end of the day, it’s one of the biggest life decisions a student will make. ENTER SALCONN. We make it our mission to make sure you don't regret your decisions in the future and are well informed of your choices. HOW? Well, We will set up a 1-on-1 communication with a trusted student advisor YOU chose from a list WE provide and worry not, this will not leave a hole in your pocket. We assure you that you will extract the maximum value for money.
